Internet of Things Research

     In Fall 2017, I participated in a group research on internet of things with Professor Mike Fudge.
    In order to better understand the concepts and application internet of things, we create a network of sensors that each connect with a Raspberry Pi to collect and exchange data. By placing them in different location of School of Information Studies, we are able to collect data about iSchool, such as loudness, temperature and humidity.
   As part of the team, I am specifically responsible for configuring Raspberry Pi to serve as a “node” on the network to collect data from sensors.
    By practicing Python language, me and my partner are able to make Raspberry Pi work by reacting to the data collected from sensors. The research is still ongoing. During the my time of research, I have gained knowledge on networking as well as Python programming skills.
