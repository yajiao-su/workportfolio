MLB Challenge
Click the image to see the complete powerpoint

I participate an all-night hack-a-thon event which we try to solve real-world problems in the sports-tech industry and pitch our ideas to current MLB.com employees. Our prompt is to come up with solution to enhance the user experience on watching the baseball game online. We decide to create an engaging on-screen jumbotron to target those who cannot make to the game but really want to engage. By adding features like music, trivia, the audience will keep their eye on the MLB game instead of changing to other channel.
