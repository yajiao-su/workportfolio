Blockchain Class
Click the image to see the whole powerpoint

   In IST 400 blockchain class, we learn about the key concepts and technology for distributed ledger technology, as well as how blokchchian revolutionize the way people live.

   Work on the team of five, we look into blockchain use cases for environments. From research, we learn that there have been country using carbon tax to incentivize environmental friendly behavior from citizens and companies. We develop the concept of GreenChain, a blockchain product and service provided to governments seeking to implement blockchain-compatible carbon tax or recycling programs.

    In order to develop GreenChain fully, we create smart contract using Ethereum to simulate the decentralized transaction between different parties. We are able to present what we learn and develop in this class to faculty and students. Below is our powerpoint.
