Information Visualization
      After a semester-long study about data science, not only I develop strong skills in coding, but also to conduct independent research with the knowledge of R, data cleaning, and Adobe Illustrator.

      My final project is to analyze the suicide rate in the world and try to find the patterns based on different factors. With the dataset that contains 43000+ rows, I clean it and analyze the dataset using R. based on the four factors: location, age, gender, change among years (1975-2015), I am able to create five graphs, which all clearly shows the result of sucide rate from different perspective.

    I then use Adobe Illustrator to design the layout of the poster to make it visually clear and attractive to the audience. Using the poster, I present my result to iSchool students and faculties.

    During the whole learning process, I enhance my knowledge of R and use it as a professional language to solve business problems.
